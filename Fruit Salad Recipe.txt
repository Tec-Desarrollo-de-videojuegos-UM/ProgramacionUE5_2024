Fruit Salad Recipe


Ingredients:


1. Mixed fruits (such as strawberries, grapes, orange, and peach)


2. Honey or Sugar for sweetness 


Steps:
1. Wash and chop fruits into bite-sized pieces.


2. Combine fruits in a bowl and drizzle with honey or sugar. 


3. Mix gently and place the bowl in the fridge for 3 hours. 




Recommendation: Serve chilled.
